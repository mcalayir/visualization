    Bad example: https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/lani.he/viz/WomeninNobelPrice/Dashboard

    I classify this as a bad visualization because it makes comparison and understanding harder than it needs to be.

    --Hard chart shape for the goal(radial design). The dashboard uses a circular/radial layout. Radial charts make people compare angles and curved distances.
